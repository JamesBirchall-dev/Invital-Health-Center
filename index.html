Our Mission

inVital Health Center and Gym was born with one sole purpose in mind, to make healthy living accessible and affordable for all and to improve the health of both body and mind to its local community.

To achieve this goal, inVital has structured a membership programme which not only caters for the die hard early and evening gym goers. It also directs huge focus in it’s off-peak time schedules making it a Health Haven for families by making the most of the day’s timetable but that’s not all….

inVital is proud that we have now launched its Health Forum, which hosts classes and discussions on how to budget a healthy diet for the whole family and allows members to share in their own successes for the benefit of the whole community.
